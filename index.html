About Me

Hello, I'm Vivian Clevinger, a college student and self-proclaimed nerd. In my free time, I like reading and writing fictional stories, ice-skating, trying new foods, and listening to music. I am also an advocate for mental health awareness, as I deal with schizoaffective disorder , which is a highly stigmatized disorder. The condition is serious, but I don't let it stop me from making the most out of my life. After being diagnosed in February 2022, I received proper treatment for my symptoms and once I became stable, I began my front-end development journey in April 2022.
